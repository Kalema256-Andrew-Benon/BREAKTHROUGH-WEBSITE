Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

+

We require basic computer literacy and a passion for technology. No prior coding experience is needed for beginner courses. For advanced courses, we recommend completing our foundational programs first.

How long do the courses take to complete?

+

Course durations vary from 8 to 12 weeks depending on the program. Most courses require 10-15 hours of commitment per week including classes and projects.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

+

Yes! Our career services team provides resume reviews, interview preparation, and connects graduates with our network of employer partners. Over 85% of our graduates find tech jobs within 6 months.

What payment options are available?

+

We offer several payment options including upfront payment, installment plans, and income share agreements where you pay after you get a job. Scholarships are also available for qualified students.
